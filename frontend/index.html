Pay with Paystack

Enter your email and amount in NGN. You'll be redirected to Paystack's secure checkout.

How this works

  1. Frontend (this page) is hosted on GitHub Pages.
  2. When you click pay, we call the Render backend to create a transaction.
  3. You are redirected to Paystack to complete payment.
  4. Paystack notifies our backend via webhooks, and you return to a success page.
Tip: Update API_BASE in app.js to your Render URL.
